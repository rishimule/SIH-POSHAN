PM Poshan Tracker


Pradhan Mantri Poshan Shakti Nirman (PM POSHAN)


OBJECTIVE
To enhance the enrollment, retention and attendance and simultaneously improve nutritional levels among school going children studying in Classes I to VIII of Government, Government - aided schools, Special Training centres (STC) and Madarasas and Maktabs supported under the Sarva Shiksha Abhiyan.

OUR SOLUTION

MDM Tracking

Real-time daily meal updates and tracking of attendance and retention of students.

Nutritional Requisite Tracking

Real time tracking of physical health of students on quarterly basis.

Calorie Tracking

Using deep learning model to detect calories and proteins through photographs.

About PM POSHAN

Pradhan Mantri Poshan Shakti Nirman or PM-POSHAN for providing one hot cooked meal in Government and Government-aided schools with the financial outlay of Rs 1.31 trillion.
