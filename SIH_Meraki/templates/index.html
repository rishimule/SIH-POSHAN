POST

POST(POshan Scheme Tracker) is our solution to track the nutrition intake of students who are provided mid day meals under the PM POSHAN Scheme.

PM POSHAN Scheme


PM POSHAN (Pradhan Mantri POshan SHAkti Nirman) Scheme is for providing one hot cooked meal in Government and Government-aided schools from 2021-22 to 2025-26, earlier known as ‘National Programme for Mid-Day Meal in Schools’ popularly known as Mid-Day Meal Scheme. This is a Centrally-Sponsored Scheme which covers all school children studying in Classes I-VIII in Government, Government-Aided Schools. The scheme covers about 11.80 crore children studying in 11.20 lakh schools across the country. 

FEATURES

MDM Tracking

Real-time daily meal updates and tracking of attendance and retention of students.

Nutritional Requisite Tracking

Tracking of physical health of students on quarterly basis.

Calorie Tracking

Using deep learning model to detect calories and proteins through photographs.
